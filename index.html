I am a researcher in intelligent construction, construction safety, computer vision, augmented reality, BIM, UAV inspection, and vision-language reasoning. My research focuses on vision-based construction safety monitoring, PPE compliance analysis, UAV-based inspection, AR-assisted defect inspection, and multimodal risk perception for work-at-height scenarios.

Research Interests

  • Vision-based construction safety monitoring

  • PPE compliance analysis for work-at-height scenarios
  • UAV-based inspection and construction site monitoring
  • Augmented reality, BIM, and digital-physical integration
  • Computer vision and vision-language reasoning in the AEC industry
